‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my first expense?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter the amount and description, then tap "Save".
How do I set up my paycheck breakdown?
Go to the Paycheck tab and enter your income and pay frequency. The app will automatically calculate your allocations.
Can I edit my savings goals?
Yes! Go to the Investments tab and tap "Edit Goal" to modify your savings target.
How do I view my spending history?
Tap "View History" on the main screen to see all your past expenses organized by date.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely using Firebase. We never share your personal financial information.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data Export to download your financial data in a portable format.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
